UI/UX Guidelines

1. Validation Errors:
   - Must be displayed in RED text.
   - Email field must validate standard email format (user@domain.com).

2. Buttons:
   - The "Pay Now" button must be GREEN.
   - "Apply" button for discount should be near the input field.

3. Success States:
   - Successful order placement shows a message in the "order-status" div.
